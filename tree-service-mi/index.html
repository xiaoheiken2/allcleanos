Fertilization & Soil Management


Healthy soil is the foundation for thriving trees and landscapes. At Bough Tree Care Services serving clients in Michigan, MI, we understand the significance that proper fertilization plays in ensuring the overall health and vigor of your trees and shrubs. Our experts conduct detailed soil analysis to determine the unique deficiencies of your plants and develop targeted fertilization plans to meet them.

While trees and shrubs often absorb nutrients naturally, soil quality, maturity, and environmental conditions can strip away essential nutrients over time. Regular fertilization renews these nutrients, promoting vigorous growth, enhanced disease resistance, and improved overall plant health. We use eco-friendly products to provide a steady supply of nutrients without the risk of leaching or burning.

Our fertilization program is designed to maintain the optimal nutrient balance, ensuring that your trees and shrubs receive the needed support at the right time. We also offer soil management services, including alkalinity control and organic matter amendment, to create the best conditions for healthy growth.
